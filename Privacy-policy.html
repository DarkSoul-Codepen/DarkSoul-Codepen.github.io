Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 01, 2024.

Hey there! We know privacy is super important, so here’s the lowdown on how we handle your information when you visit DarkSoul CodePen.

What We Collect

When you drop by our site, we gather some info about your device automatically. This includes your IP address, browser type, internet service provider, referring/exit pages, your operating system, date/time stamps, and related data. We call this “Device Information,” and we collect it using cookies, log files, and other tracking tools.

Cookies

Yep, we use cookies! They help us understand how you interact with our site and serve up relevant ads.

GitHub Cookies

Since our site is hosted on GitHub, they use cookies to keep your browsing secure and smooth. These cookies are essential for enhancing security, gathering insights, and supporting future developments. For more details, check out GitHub Cookie Policy and GitHub Privacy Statement.

Google Adsense

We also work with third-party vendors like Google, which use cookies to show you ads based on your past visits. These cookies let Google and its partners present ads that match your interests across the web. You can tweak your ad settings by visiting Google’s Ad Settings.

Why We Use Your Information

We use the info we collect to:

  • Analyze how you use our site
  • Fix technical issues
  • Improve our services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to get a better idea of how you’re using our site. Want to know more? Check out Google's Privacy & Terms. If you prefer to opt-out, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Microsoft Clarity

We also use Microsoft Clarity to see how you interact with our site, using tools like heatmaps and session replays. This helps us improve your experience and keep things secure. Learn more in the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

No Sharing Your Information

We don’t sell or trade your personal info to outside parties. We might share it with trusted partners who help us run our site and services, but they’re committed to keeping your data safe. We might also release your info to comply with legal requirements or protect our rights and safety.

Your Rights

If you’re in Europe, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and ask for it to be corrected, updated, or deleted. Just reach out to us using the contact details below.

Changes

We might update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Keep an eye on this page for any updates.

Contact Us

Got questions or concerns about your privacy? Feel free to email us at darksoul.codepen@gmail.com or visit the Contact page on our website.

Thanks for stopping by and happy coding!
